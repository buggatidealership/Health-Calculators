<h2>FAQ</h2>
<div class="faq-section">
  <h3><HelpCircle className="section-icon" />Frequently Asked Questions</h3>

  <div class="faq-item">
    <h4>How accurate are height predictors?</h4>
    <p>They range in accuracy. Most methods predict within ±2–3 inches (5–7.5cm).</p>
  </div>

  <div class="faq-item">
    <h4>Which method is the most accurate?</h4>
    <p>The Khamis-Roche method is among the most accurate without needing bone scans, with ~80% of predictions falling within 5cm of adult height.</p>
  </div>

  <div class="faq-item">
    <h4>Can growth spurts change predictions?</h4>
    <p>Yes. Puberty timing can temporarily distort projections. Periodic re-evaluation is recommended.</p>
  </div>
</div>
