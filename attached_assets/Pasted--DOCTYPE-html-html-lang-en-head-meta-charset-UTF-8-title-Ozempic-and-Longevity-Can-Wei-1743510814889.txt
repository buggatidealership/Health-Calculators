Experts emphasize incorporating strength training and adequate protein intake while on Ozempic&#8203;:contentReference[oaicite:33]{index=33}&#8203;:contentReference[oaicite:34]{index=34}. Building or at least maintaining muscle through resistance exercise can counteract the drug’s muscle-wasting side effect. Always review the full list of side effects and warnings with your healthcare provider.</p> <p>For longevity-focused users, one side effect to watch is any sign of frailty or over-fatigue, which could indicate you’re losing weight too fast or not getting enough nutrients. It’s generally not advisable to have more than a 1-2 pound per week weight loss sustained over long periods, as very rapid loss can strain the body. Using the lowest effective dose of Ozempic that achieves steady progress is a prudent strategy. Remember, slow and steady weight loss can be healthier and easier to maintain than a sudden drastic drop.</p> <h3>3. Long-Term Use and Unknowns</h3> <p>Can you take Ozempic indefinitely?
