  <h2>How to Use the Caloric & Macronutrient Calculator</h2>
  <p>This calculator helps you estimate your total daily energy expenditure (TDEE) and your ideal macronutrient breakdown for weight loss, maintenance, or muscle gain.</p>

  <ul style="margin-top: 20px; padding-left: 20px;">
    <li><strong>Gender:</strong> Impacts your basal metabolic rate (BMR) due to body composition differences.</li>
    <li><strong>Age:</strong> Affects how many calories you burn at rest.</li>
    <li><strong>Weight & Height:</strong> Used to calculate your BMR using the Mifflin-St Jeor equation.</li>
    <li><strong>Activity Level:</strong> Multiplies BMR to estimate TDEE — the more active, the more energy you burn.</li>
    <li><strong>Goal:</strong> Adjusts calories to create a deficit or surplus for fat loss or muscle gain.</li>
    <li><strong>Diet Preference:</strong> Allows selection of macronutrient strategy (balanced, low-carb, high-carb, or keto).</li>
  </ul>

  <p style="margin-top: 20px;">
    Estimates are based on evidence-backed guidelines from the ISSN and Academy of Nutrition and Dietetics.
  </p>
