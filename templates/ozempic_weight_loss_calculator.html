Frequently Asked Questions

How much weight can I lose on Ozempic?

Most people lose between 5% and 15% of their body weight. Results depend on your dose, duration, and lifestyle.

When will I start seeing results?

Most people start noticing weight loss within 4–6 weeks. Weight loss continues steadily throughout treatment.

Should I diet while taking Ozempic?

Yes, combining Ozempic with a healthy diet increases weight loss significantly.

Will I regain weight after stopping Ozempic?

Many people regain weight if they stop Ozempic without maintaining diet and exercise changes.

Is this calculator based on real clinical trials?

Yes, it's based on STEP and SUSTAIN clinical trials published in medical journals.
