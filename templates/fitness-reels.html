Domain #5 · Cold Start Experiment

Fitness Reels

3 reels built from whitespace research. Each uses a format imported from another domain — none of these formats are standard in fitness content.

From: Finance
Data Reveal
Number as hook → mechanism → implication. Protein timing shown on a clock face. The viewer sees their own mistake before being told.
"The moment the meals redistribute on the clock."
From: Science Communication
I Was Wrong
Self-correction format. Three beliefs get redacted with evidence underneath. Trust through vulnerability — the creator is the myth-holder.
"The moment the old belief gets crossed out."
From: Career/Professional
Audience Cohort
"If you're over 40 and just started lifting." Training profile as a patient file. Specificity is the hook — the viewer recognizes themselves.
"The moment 'over 40' appears and they stop scrolling."
