How to Use the Ozempic Pen Click Calculator

This calculator helps you understand your Ozempic dose based on pen clicks. Each pen type has a specific click value.

  • Select your pen type (Blue 0.25-0.5mg, Gray 1-2mg, or Green 2-4mg)
  • Enter the number of clicks you've dialed on your pen's dose selector
  • Add your last injection date (optional) to calculate your next scheduled dose
  • Click "Calculate Dose" to see your dose in milligrams and safety information

Ozempic Pen Click Reference

  • Blue Pen (0.25-0.5mg): Each click = 0.0625mg (4 clicks = 0.25mg)
  • Gray Pen (1-2mg): Each click = 0.125mg (4 clicks = 0.5mg)
  • Green Pen (2-4mg): Each click = 0.25mg (4 clicks = 1mg)

These values are based on official Ozempic dosing specifications.

Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are Ozempic pen clicks?

Each Ozempic pen has a specific number of clicks per dose. The blue pen (0.25-0.5mg) has 4 clicks per 0.25mg. The gray pen (1-2mg) has 4 clicks per 0.5mg. The green pen (2-4mg) has 4 clicks per 1mg.

For accurate dosing, make sure each click is complete—you should hear and feel a distinct click. The dose window should display the correct dose amount.

Can I use this with Wegovy or other GLP-1 pens?

No. This calculator is designed specifically for Ozempic pens. Other GLP-1 medication pens like Wegovy, Rybelsus, or Mounjaro may have different click calibrations and dosing mechanisms.

Always use the correct calculator for your specific medication, and follow your healthcare provider's dosing instructions.

What happens if I count clicks incorrectly?

Miscounting clicks could lead to incorrect dosing. Underdosing may reduce medication effectiveness, while overdosing could increase side effects like nausea, vomiting, or diarrhea.

Always verify your dose in the pen's dose window. If you're unsure, contact your healthcare provider for guidance.

How do I know which Ozempic pen I have?

Ozempic pens are color-coded:

  • Blue pen: 0.25mg or 0.5mg doses (lower doses for starting therapy)
  • Gray pen: 1mg or 2mg doses (maintenance therapy)
  • Green pen: 2mg or 4mg doses (higher doses for some patients)

The pen color and dose range are printed on the pen label and packaging.
