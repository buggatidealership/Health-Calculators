Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is this calculator?

It provides an estimate based on manufacturer click spacing. Always verify with your pharmacist or prescriber.

Can I use this for Wegovy or Mounjaro?

No. This calculator is calibrated for Ozempic pens only. Use specific tools for other GLP-1 injectables.

What if I miscount the clicks?

Start from zero after hearing the first audible click. Count slowly and steadily to avoid errors.

How do I know which pen I have?

Ozempic pens are color-coded: Blue (0.25-0.5mg), Gray (1-2mg), or Green (2-4mg). The pen type is printed on the label and packaging.

Why doesn't my click count match my prescribed dose?

Healthcare providers sometimes prescribe non-standard doses that require a specific number of clicks. Always follow your provider's instructions regarding the exact number of clicks for your dose.
