What Is Implant Profile?

Profile = how far the implant sticks out. Two 350cc implants can look very different based on whether they're low, moderate, or high profile.

Example: A 300cc high-profile implant has less width but more forward projection than a moderate profile one.

Visual Comparison of Profiles

Surgeon Tips by Frame Type

  • Petite: Under 5'4", bands 30–32 → Avoid high profile. Moderate is ideal.
  • Average: 32–34 bands → Moderate or high depending on goal
  • Wide Frame: 36+ bands → Low profile can give better lateral fill

FAQs

What is implant profile?

It describes the height or projection of an implant, independent of volume (cc).

Does profile affect sizing?

Yes. High profile looks fuller from the side. Low profile appears flatter but wider.

Can I choose my profile?

Yes, with surgeon input. Choice depends on anatomy, lifestyle, and aesthetic goals.
