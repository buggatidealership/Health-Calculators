How to Use the Ozempic Weight Loss Calculator

Use clinical trial data, personalized metrics, and smart habits to maximize your results while on semaglutide (Ozempic/Wegovy).

The Ozempic Weight Loss Calculator gives you a personalized estimate of how much weight you could lose while on semaglutide. This guide will help you interpret your results, understand the science behind the numbers, and give you practical tips to improve your outcomes.

1. How the Calculator Works

  • Based on STEP 1–4 clinical trial data (NEJM, 2021)
  • Adjusts for dosage (0.5mg, 1mg, 2.4mg)
  • Projects weight loss over 20–68 weeks
  • Factors in baseline BMI and starting weight

2. What Your Results Mean

  • Projected Fat Loss: Based on weekly dosage and clinical averages
  • Expected BMI Drop: Visualized change from starting BMI to projected BMI
  • Final Weight: Estimated based on treatment length and adherence

3. How to Maximize Weight Loss While on Ozempic

  • Eat 1.2–1.6g protein per kg of body weight daily
  • Strength train at least 2x per week to preserve lean mass
  • Sleep 7–9 hours nightly and manage stress
  • Hydrate with 2.5–3.5L water daily

4. How Long Should You Stay on Ozempic?

The STEP trials lasted 68 weeks. Most users see results between weeks 8–20, with continued progress beyond that. Long-term use is generally safe when monitored by a physician, but combining Ozempic with lasting lifestyle changes is key to maintaining results.

5. Can You Regain Weight After Stopping?

Yes. Many people regain 30–60% of the weight lost unless habits are improved. Focus on sustainable nutrition and physical activity so the progress becomes permanent.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use this calculator for Wegovy or Rybelsus?

Yes. Wegovy and Ozempic use semaglutide; the calculator applies to both. Rybelsus is the oral version and results may vary slightly.

What's the best dose for weight loss?

2.4mg (Wegovy) showed the highest results in STEP 1, with average 15% body weight loss at 68 weeks.

Can you build muscle while on Ozempic?

Yes, but you need adequate protein and resistance training. The drug reduces appetite, so make sure to track macros.

Will I gain weight back after stopping?

Without lifestyle changes, some weight regain is expected. This guide and the calculator aim to support sustainable progress.
