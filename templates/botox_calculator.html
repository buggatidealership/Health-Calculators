How This Calculator Works

This calculator combines evidence-based clinical guidelines from medical literature with real-world practice patterns to estimate your Botox dosage and cost. Here's what's happening behind the scenes:

Dosage Calculation Factors

  • Treatment Area: Different facial areas require different unit amounts, with larger muscles like masseter (jaw) needing more units than smaller areas like bunny lines.
  • Gender: Men typically require 20-30% more units due to stronger facial muscles.
  • Treatment Intensity: Light treatments use minimum effective doses for subtle results, while advanced treatments use maximum recommended doses for dramatic results.

Cost Estimation Methodology

  • Geographic Variation: Prices are adjusted based on average regional costs in your selected country.
  • Provider Type: Dermatologists and plastic surgeons typically charge premium rates, while medical spas may offer more competitive pricing.
  • Currency Conversion: All calculations are performed in USD then converted to local currency for accuracy.

All dosing guidelines are based on published medical literature and clinical consensus, but individual results and recommendations may vary.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long will my Botox results last?
Most Botox treatments last 3-4 months for first-time patients. Regular users may experience longer-lasting results (4-6 months) as muscles become conditioned over time. Results vary based on metabolism, treatment area, and dosage.
Is this calculator accurate for all Botox brands?
This calculator provides estimates based on Botox® (onabotulinumtoxinA) specifically. Other neuromodulators like Dysport®, Xeomin®, or Jeuveau® may require different dosing. Dysport, for example, typically requires 2.5-3x more units than Botox for equivalent results.
Why do men need more Botox than women?
Men typically have stronger facial muscles with greater mass and density than women. This increases the amount of neurotoxin needed to achieve the same level of muscle relaxation. Our calculator automatically adjusts dosage recommendations by 20-30% for male patients.
