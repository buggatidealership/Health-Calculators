How this is calculated

Click counts are derived directly from the Novo Nordisk Ozempic prescribing information and pen user guides. Each pen delivers a fixed volume per click — the calculator maps your selected dose to the exact number of clicks for that pen's concentration.

The 2mg pen (blue) has a different concentration than the 4mg (gray) and 8mg (green) pens, which is why the same dose requires different click counts on different pens.
