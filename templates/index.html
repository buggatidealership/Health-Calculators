Understanding Macronutrients

Protein: Essential for muscle repair and growth. Each gram provides 4 calories.

Carbohydrates: Your body's primary energy source. Each gram provides 4 calories.

Fats: Important for hormone production and vitamin absorption. Each gram provides 9 calories.

Why Different Diet Types?

Different macronutrient ratios work better for different individuals based on their goals, preferences, and metabolic responses:

  • Balanced: A traditional approach with moderate amounts of all macronutrients.
  • Low-Carb: Reduces carbohydrates while increasing fat and protein, which may help with hunger control.
  • High-Carb: Favored by many endurance athletes for increased glycogen storage.
  • Keto: Very low carbohydrate, high fat diet that shifts your body to primarily burning fat for fuel.
