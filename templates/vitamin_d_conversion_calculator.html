Frequently Asked Questions

What is the conversion factor?

1 ng/mL = 2.5 nmol/L and 1 nmol/L = 0.4 ng/mL

Which unit does my lab use?

Most US labs report vitamin D in ng/mL, while international reports use nmol/L.

What is a healthy vitamin D level?

Generally, 30–50 ng/mL (75–125 nmol/L) is considered adequate by many health organizations.
