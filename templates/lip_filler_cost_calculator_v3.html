Methodology

This calculator uses a base price of $684/syringe from ASPS 2023 data, adjusted by three multipliers: geographic location (0.6-1.0x), provider experience (0.6-1.4x), and product type (0.8-1.3x). Touch-up costs are a percentage of initial treatment cost. Annual estimates assume 1.5-2.4 treatments/year based on typical HA filler longevity of 4-12 months in lips.

Lip filler costs an average of $684 per syringe in the United States (ASPS 2023). Most first-time patients need 1 syringe (1ml). Total cost ranges from $500 to $2,000+ depending on provider type, geographic location, and filler brand. Lip fillers typically last 4-12 months, requiring 1-2 annual maintenance treatments. Board-certified providers charge more but have lower complication rates. HA (hyaluronic acid) fillers are the most common type and are reversible with hyaluronidase.
