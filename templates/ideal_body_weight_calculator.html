How to Use the Ideal Body Weight Calculator

Follow these steps to calculate your ideal body weight based on medical formulas:

  1. Enter your height in feet/inches or centimeters.
  2. Select your biological sex.
  3. Optionally choose your frame size (small, medium, large).
  4. Choose a formula (e.g., Devine, Robinson, Miller).
  5. Click "Estimate" to get your ideal body weight.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is ideal body weight (IBW)?

IBW is a medically recommended target weight based on height and gender, often used in clinical nutrition and drug dosing.

Which formula is most accurate?

Devine is widely used in hospitals; Robinson and Miller are alternatives. They yield slightly different results.

Should I use frame size?

Frame size can refine estimates but is optional. Use wrist circumference to estimate frame size if needed.

Is this the same as BMI?

No. BMI assesses weight relative to height, while IBW tries to pinpoint the healthiest baseline weight.

Can I use this for children?

No. This tool is for adults. Use pediatric growth calculators for children.
