What Are Starbucks Drink Nutrition Facts?

Starbucks drink nutrition varies dramatically by size, milk choice, and syrup pumps. A Grande Caffe Latte has 190 calories with 2% milk but drops to 130 with almond milk. This calculator uses official Starbucks nutrition data to compute accurate calorie, sugar, protein, carb, and fat values for any drink customization.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where does the nutrition data come from?

It's based on the official Starbucks menu, nutrition PDFs, and publicly available product data. We update frequently to reflect seasonal offerings.

Does this include modifiers like sweet cream or foam?

Yes, most of them are supported. Toppings like cold foam or whipped cream are optional toggles and update macros in real time.

Can I use this for keto or low-carb tracking?

Yes — use it to remove classic syrup and swap to sugar-free or no milk. You'll see carbs and sugar drop significantly.

How accurate is it?

Values are based on standard sizes and ingredient servings. Customizations made by baristas may slightly vary. Use this as a close approximation.
