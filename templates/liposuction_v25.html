Methodology

This calculator estimates fat removal volume using BMI-stratified ranges and number of treatment areas. Normal BMI patients: 1-3L, overweight: 2-4L, obese: 3-5L, with a 5L safety cap per ASPS guidelines. Fat weight uses adipose density of 0.9 kg/L. Cost is per-area with regional base prices ($1,500-$2,500) and a 10% male surcharge.

Liposuction safely removes 2-5 liters of fat per outpatient procedure. This equals roughly 4-11 pounds of fat tissue. The ASPS safety limit is 5 liters. Cost ranges from $2,000-$3,500 per area in the US. Liposuction is body contouring, not weight loss -- expect 5-10 pounds of actual weight change but significant improvements in body contour and measurements.
