Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the implant volume estimate?

We use cup size difference and base width to calculate a starting range. Always confirm with your surgeon using sizers and 3D imaging.

What does implant profile mean?

Implant profile refers to the forward projection off the chest wall. Profiles vary from low to ultra-high and depend on your chest width and goals.

How much do implants weigh?

Each implant adds 0.5–1.5 pounds. Silicone and gummy bear implants are heavier than saline for the same volume.

Will implants affect my ability to breastfeed?

It depends on placement and incision. Many women can still breastfeed, but subglandular placement or periareolar incisions may pose risks.
