What Are Chipotle's Nutrition Facts?

Chipotle meals are built from individual ingredients, each with published nutrition data. A typical burrito bowl ranges from 400-1,100 calories depending on protein, rice, and topping choices. This calculator sums per-ingredient nutrition values from Chipotle's official data to give you accurate calorie, protein, carb, and fat totals for any custom order.

Medical Disclaimer: This tool provides educational estimates only. Nutrition values are based on Chipotle's published data but may vary by location or preparation. For medical concerns, consult a registered dietitian.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where does the nutrition data come from?

All nutrition data is based on Chipotle's official ingredient and allergen guide, as well as USDA food database entries.

Can I use this to track macros or cut calories?

Yes — you can instantly adjust ingredients and see how your Chipotle meal fits into your macro goals.

Are vegan and vegetarian options supported?

Yes, you can customize with sofritas, black beans, fajita veggies, guacamole, and more.

Does this calculator work for delivery orders?

Yes — ingredient macros match in-store and digital orders. Custom portioning may vary slightly.
