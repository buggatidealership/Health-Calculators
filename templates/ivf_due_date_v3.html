Methodology

This calculator uses IVF-specific gestational dating formulas aligned with ACOG Committee Opinion No. 700 (2017). The core formula is:

Due Date = Transfer Date + (266 − Embryo Age)

Where 266 represents the standard 38-week (266-day) gestation from conception, and Embryo Age is the number of days the embryo developed before transfer (3, 5, or 6 days).

For egg retrieval-based calculations, the due date is Retrieval Date + 266 days for Day 3 embryos or Retrieval Date + 264 days for Day 5 blastocysts, since retrieval approximates fertilization day.

Gestational age uses LMP-equivalent dating: GA = Days Since Transfer + Embryo Age + 14. The 14-day offset converts from conception-based dating to the clinical standard that counts from the last menstrual period. This means at transfer, a Day 5 embryo is already at 2 weeks 5 days gestational age.

Only about 5% of babies arrive on their exact due date. This tool provides clinical estimates and does not replace dating performed by your reproductive endocrinologist or OB-GYN.
