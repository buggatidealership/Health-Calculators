How much water with creatine?

Personalized hydration for your protocol

Creatine Hydration Calculator

Your Recommended Water Intake:

3.5 liters per day (118 oz)

During the maintenance phase, take 3-5g of creatine monohydrate daily.

This is 1.2 liters more than the typical recommendation for someone with your body weight not using creatine.

Hydration Tips:

  • Spread your water intake evenly throughout the day
  • Monitor urine color - aim for pale yellow
  • Take your creatine dose with at least 8 oz (240ml) of water
  • Maintain consistent hydration to maximize muscle gains
  • Avoid excessive caffeine as it may counteract hydration

Note: This calculator provides estimates based on recommendations from the International Society of Sports Nutrition (ISSN) and other research on creatine supplementation and hydration requirements.
