Methodology

This calculator uses 7 validated 1RM prediction formulas: Epley (1985), Brzycki (1993), Lander (1985), Lombardi (1989), Mayhew (1992), OConner (1989), and Wathen (1994). The average of all 7 is displayed as the primary estimate. Training load percentages follow NSCA guidelines. Most accurate in the 2-10 rep range.

One rep max (1RM) is the maximum weight you can lift for one repetition. The Epley formula estimates it as: 1RM = Weight x (1 + Reps/30). For example, benching 225 lbs for 5 reps gives an estimated 1RM of about 263 lbs. Training zones: 85% for strength (6 reps), 75% for hypertrophy (10 reps), 65% for endurance (15+ reps).
