  • A Grande Caffe Latte with 2% milk has about 190 calories, 18g sugar, 7g fat, and 13g protein. Switch to nonfat to save ~20 calories.

  • Grande Americano (225mg), followed by Cold Brew (205mg) and drip coffee. Frappuccinos have surprisingly low caffeine (95mg).

  • A Grande Coffee Frappuccino has about 54g sugar (nearly 14 teaspoons). That's more sugar than a can of Coca-Cola (39g).

Starbucks drink calories range from 5 (black cold brew) to 500+ (Frappuccino with whipped cream). A Grande Caffe Latte has 190 cal, 18g sugar. Switching from 2% to oat milk adds about 40 cal. Each syrup pump adds 20 cal and 5g sugar. Whipped cream adds 80 cal and 8g fat. The lowest-calorie options are Americano (15 cal), Cold Brew (5 cal), and plain iced coffee (5 cal).
