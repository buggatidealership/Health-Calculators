Methodology

Dosing uses ISSN Position Stand (Kreider et al., 2017). Loading: 0.3 g/kg/day for 5-7 days split into 4 doses. Maintenance: 3-5 g/day (0.03-0.04 g/kg/day). Strength athletes get the higher end. 1 teaspoon = approximately 5g creatine monohydrate.

The ISSN recommends 3-5 grams of creatine monohydrate daily for maintenance. For a 77 kg (170 lb) person, this is about 4-5 grams per day (roughly 1 teaspoon). An optional loading phase of 20g/day (split into 4 doses) for 5-7 days saturates stores faster. No cycling is needed. Long-term use up to 5 years is safe for healthy individuals. Creatine is the most studied supplement in sports nutrition with over 500 peer-reviewed studies.
