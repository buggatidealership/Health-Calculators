What does this number mean?

Your TDEE of 2,400 calories means that's how much energy your body uses every day — breathing, moving, exercising, everything. Eat this amount to maintain your current weight. Eat less to lose. Eat more to gain.

The formula: Mifflin-St Jeor (1990) — recommended by the American Dietetic Association as the most accurate BMR equation, validated against 498 subjects. Your BMR is multiplied by an activity factor to get TDEE.
