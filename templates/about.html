About HealthCalculators.xyz

Free, evidence-based health calculators built on peer-reviewed research and clinical guidelines.

Our Mission

HealthCalculators.xyz provides free, accurate health and wellness calculators that help people make informed decisions about their bodies. Every calculator on this site is built using formulas derived from peer-reviewed medical research, clinical guidelines, and established health standards.

We believe health information should be accessible, transparent, and grounded in science — not marketing or speculation. Our tools are designed to give you a starting point for understanding your health, not to replace professional medical advice.

Our Methodology

Each calculator on this site follows a consistent development process:

  • Research-first design: We identify the established medical formulas, clinical guidelines, or peer-reviewed studies that underpin each calculation before writing a single line of code.
  • Transparent formulas: Where possible, we cite the specific studies, formulas, or guidelines our calculators use (e.g., Mifflin-St Jeor for TDEE, Army AR 600-9 for body fat, STEP trials for Ozempic projections).
  • Conservative estimates: When clinical data shows a range, we default to conservative projections rather than optimistic ones.
  • Limitations stated: Every calculator includes a disclaimer noting what it can and cannot tell you, and when you should consult a healthcare professional.

What We Cover

Our calculator library spans several health and wellness categories:

  • Nutrition & Diet: TDEE, caloric intake, macronutrient planning, carb cycling, and restaurant-specific nutrition trackers (Chipotle, Starbucks)
  • Weight Management: Fasting weight loss, ideal body weight, GLP-1/Ozempic projections
  • Cosmetic & Surgical: Breast implant sizing and cost, Botox dosage, lip filler cost, liposuction estimates
  • Fertility & Pregnancy: IVF due dates, female fertility windows, child growth tracking, newborn weight, dog pregnancy
  • General Health: BAC estimation, alcohol impact, vitamin D intake, creatine hydration, lipid panel goals, baldness risk, adult height prediction
  • Financial: Plasma donation earnings, retirement savings

Medical Disclaimer

The calculators and articles on this site are for informational and educational purposes only. They are not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult a qualified healthcare provider before making health decisions based on calculator results.

Our tools use population-level data and general formulas. Individual results vary based on genetics, medical history, medications, and other factors that no online calculator can fully account for.
