## Emotional State / Mental Model
- **Primary motivation:** Financial/practical. Users are looking for extra income, not health optimization.
- **User archetypes:** College students needing side money, gig workers supplementing income, people between jobs, those exploring passive income streams.
- **Emotional tone:** Pragmatic, slightly anxious ("will I qualify?"), hopeful ("how much can I actually make?").
- **Key concern:** "Is it worth my time?" — they're doing a cost-benefit analysis (time spent vs money earned).
- **Mental model:** Think of it like a side job. They want to know: how much per hour effectively, not just per donation.
- **Language patterns:** "make money," "earn," "get paid," "worth it," "per month," "side hustle."

## Competitor Data Points (from existing calculator + industry knowledge)
- **Per donation:** $20-60 regular donors, $40-100 new donors (first 8-10 donations)
- **New donor bonuses:** $200-1,000+ for first month across major centers
- **Frequency:** FDA allows up to 2x/week (7-day period), at least 1 day between
- **Weight matters:** 175+ lbs = higher volume = $5-15 more per donation
- **Time per visit:** 1-1.5 hours (return), 2-3 hours (first visit)
- **Major centers:** BioLife, CSL Plasma, Grifols, Octapharma
- **State variation:** CA, NY, TX pay most; rural states pay less
- **Annual max:** ~104 donations/year at 2x/week
