### HIGH-PERFORMANCE ATOMS (>20% of top tweets):
1. **Direct address to reader** (52%)
2. **Absurd/extreme scenarios** (38%)
3. **Moral contradiction exposure** (31%)
4. **Cultural critique without explanation** (29%)
5. **Single-sentence declarative statements** (45%)

### RARE BUT POWERFUL ATOMS (<5% but high engagement):
1. **"Worthless" as descriptor** (3 tweets, avg 45k+ likes)
2. **Meta-commentary on discourse itself** (8 tweets, 25k+ avg)
3. **Specific numerical comparisons** (12 tweets, 30k+ avg)

**KEY INSIGHT**: The highest-performing tweets combine moral/cultural outrage with simple, direct language and often use "imagine" to force readers into uncomfortable perspective-taking scenarios.
